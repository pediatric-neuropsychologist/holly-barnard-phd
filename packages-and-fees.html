Additional Services

All additional psychologist services provided by Dr. Barnard, such as IEP meeting attendance, therapy, and teaching, are billed at an hourly rate of $200/hour, including travel. An initial deposit of $200 will be due at the time of the Diagnostic Interview and will be applied towards the cost of the full evaluation. 50% of the remaining balance will be due on the day of Diagnostic Testing, and the final 50% will be due at the time of the Feedback Session. Dr. Barnard accepts Zelle, checks, and credit card payments. An additional fee will be assessed for credit card transactions.

Insurance

In order to provide patients and their families with the attention and care they deserve, as well as the freedom to fully explore all diagnoses that may be impacting the patient, Dr. Barnard does not bill insurance companies directly and she is not on any insurance panels.

Dr. Barnard can provide a statement that details appointments, charges, and billing codes should one wish to file with insurance for out-of-network benefits for services. If you plan to submit for reimbursement, please contact your insurance company to get information about your plan's benefits.
